Shengfang ZHAI

Research Fellow @ National University of Singapore

I am currently a Research Fellow at the National University of Singapore (NUS), working with Prof. Jiaheng Zhang. I also collaborate closely with Prof. Hang Su and Prof. Yinpeng Dong from Tsinghua University.

I received my Ph.D. degree from Peking University, fortunately advised by Prof. Qingni Shen. And I was a visiting Ph.D. student at the Nanyang Technological University, advised by Prof. Yang Liu from December 2023 to August 2024.

My research mainly focuses on Trustworthy AI. Specifically, I focus on red-teaming (privacy violations / backdoor attacks / alignment bypasses) cutting-edge foundation models (Diffusion/LLM) and agentic systems, and further designing targeted mechanisms to enhance safe generation.

Feel free to drop me an email if you are interested in our work, and I am always willing to work together on interesting projects with external collaborators.
